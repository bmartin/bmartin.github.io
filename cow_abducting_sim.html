Cow Abducting Simulator

Mouse:
  • Click to move ship.
  • Click on ship for tractor beam.
Keyboard:
  • Use arrow keys or "w", "a", "d" to control the rockets yourself.
  • Use down arrow or "s" for tractor beam.
  • Use "space" to drop item.
  • Use "1" to drop hay.
  • Use "2" to drop a cow.
  • Press "Esc" to pause.

Try moving your mouse around and clicking. It'll get more fun when we add cows. :)
